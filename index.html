My Story

I don't just write code,
I build solutions.

My journey into the world of tech isn't a traditional one. Back in 2021, while pursuing a degree in Botany, I discovered a profound passion for coding during my free time. What started as sheer curiosity—tinkering with web structures without a formal guide—quickly evolved into a relentless drive to create.

I am a proud self-taught freelancer. I didn't have a structured roadmap, but I had the persistence to debug, learn, and grow. From mastering intuitive UI/UX designs and modern front-end frameworks to currently expanding my skills into Python, my entire skill set is fueled purely by the love for the craft.

I believe that the best way to learn is by doing. That's why I focus on building real-world applications that solve actual problems. Whether you need a simple landing page or a complex digital solution, I bring dedication, self-driven expertise, and a unique perspective to every project.
